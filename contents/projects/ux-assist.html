--- country: India date: 2016-01-01 projectUrl: http://uxassist.com title: UX Assist ---

Adit created UX Assist for iOS. It’s the first product of its kind that has grouped UX activities into 12 unique workflows based on the kind of product you’re working on, namely, an optimum viable product, minimum viable product, client-based product and a data-driven product. Each product has a different UX design lifecycle. The app gives user, task based recommendations for each product types. It will assists users in making your their own workflow based on the business requirements.
